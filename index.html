Warranty

Admin fills out warranty form
(from Corporate Help Desk)

Admin then schedules time that works for the homeowner of builder
(Pending)

Technician is then assigned and goes out to the house
(Field Tech Review Needed)

Technician fills out the portal selecting a subcontractor is needed and the payment. They also decide if it is billable and any materials need to be ordered. Finally they upload images and check Field Tech Reviewed
(Field Tech Review Needed)

If job is not billable and materials are needed
Status becomes:
Material Purchase Needed

If job is billable and materials or subcontractor are needed
Status becomes:
Customer Review Needed

If no materials are needed and job is non-billable
Status becomes:
Scheduled - Awaiting Field

Once job is completed, the field tech logs in, navigates to Scheduled - Awaiting Field, adds pictures, updates the subcontractor, payment amount, vendor if needed, and checks Job Completed.

Next, admin reaches out again to schedule a follow-up appointment. Either the field tech or subcontractor goes to fix the house.
Status: Scheduled - Awaiting Field

If a subcontractor was needed, the status goes to
Subcontractor To Pay

Finally, if it was billable, the status goes to
Ready To Invoice
